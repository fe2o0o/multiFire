About Us

Multi Fire Internatinal Company

Eng : Wael Abu Al-Enein

Multi Fire Engineering Industries Company specializes in all electromechanical installations for firefighting works, including fire line installations and related fire boxes of 2.5 inches and 1 inches, and splinters for extinguishing with water according to the Egyptian code and the required technical specifications. Supplying and installing all fire pumps (jockey - (diesel - electricity), fire pipes, alarm network installations complete with its own electrical panels, supply and installation of all types of extinguishing devices with different capacities, whether dry chemical powder, foam, carbon dioxide or FM200 gas, kitchen extinguishing systems, spraying fire retardant materials and Chemical treatment against fire hazards. There is also maintenance of fire engines, pumps, and all these systems and networks of all kinds
